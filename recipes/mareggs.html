Mayak Eggs (Korean Marinated Egg)

Mayak Gyeran which is the korean name for this dish translates to "drug eggs". This simple to made recipe is incredibly delcious and has a very addictive taste. These soft, medium, or hard boiled eggs, depending on how you like to cook them, are marinated in a variety of sauces and spices to create a very addcitive flavor.

Ingredients

  • Medium-Large Container
  • Choice of eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Honey
  • Water
  • Onion
  • Green Onion
  • Chili pepper
  • garlic
  • Gochuraru or chilli powder
  • Sesame seeds

How to . . .

Perfectly cook a hard boiled egg

  1. Fill a correct sized pot with enough water to submerge your eggs but will not overflow when you add eggs and when water boils
  2. Heat water to boil then lower to rapid simmer
  3. Place eggs gently
  4. Cook for 9-15 minutes depending what egg size you have
  5. Place eggs in ice water bath
  6. Peel Eggs

    7. You have successfully cooked hard boiled eggs, nice!

Perfectly cook a soft boiled egg or jammy eggs

  1. Fill a correct sized pot with enough water to submerge your eggs but will not overflow when you add eggs and when water boils
  2. Heat water to boil then lower to rapid simmer
  3. Place eggs gently
  4. Cook for exactly 6-7 minutes! (6 minutes for more runnier yolk)
  5. Immediately put eggs in ice water bath
  6. Peel Eggs

    7. You have successfully cooked soft boiled eggs, nice chef!

Perfectly cook a medium boiled

  1. Fill a correct sized pot with enough water to submerge your eggs but will not overflow when you add eggs and when water boils
  2. Heat water to boil then lower to rapid simmer
  3. Place eggs gently
  4. Cook for 7-9 minutes depending what egg size you have
  5. Place eggs in ice water bath
  6. Peel Eggs

    7. You have successfully cooked medium boiled eggs, wooo!

Time to Marinate!

To make the marinade is pretty straight forward

  1. Chop the vegetables from the ingredients list.
  2. Put all vegetables, sauces, and spices all to your medium-large container.
  3. Add water so there is enough to submerge egg but not to overflow when adding them in
  4. Give marinade a good mix
  5. Add in your choice of egg
  6. Close container and let eggs marinate for a minimum of 8 hours or over night. (Tip: Place paper towel ontop of container under the lid so that it will completely submerge the egg so the entirety of the egg is marinted.)
  7. Place egg and pour some sauce over some steamy white rice and enjoy!
  8. Get addicted
